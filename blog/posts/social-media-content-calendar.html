Build a Social Content Calendar That Drives Leads

Posting consistently is not enough. A high-performance calendar links each post to a business objective: awareness, trust, demand capture, or conversion.

Define content pillars around your offer

Create 3-4 pillars such as education, proof, behind-the-scenes, and direct offer promotion. This keeps messaging balanced and prevents repetitive posts.

Plan weekly cadence and formats

Assign a realistic publishing rhythm: for example, two short videos, one carousel, and one authority post per week. Repetition in format improves execution speed and quality.

Weekly optimization loop

  • Review top-performing hooks
  • Analyze saves, shares, and DM replies
  • Recycle winners with new angles

Link content to your funnel

Every week should include one clear CTA pathway: booking call, lead magnet, or service inquiry. Without this, reach increases but lead flow remains flat.
